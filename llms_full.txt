SECTION 5: CORE PATENTS & LIFE-SAVING / REVENUE INNOVATIONS
================================================================

------------------------------------------------
Patent IN358472 — QuickTunes™ (Filed 2017, Granted 2021)
------------------------------------------------

QuickTunes is a life-saving AI driving safety platform that discourages
drivers from picking up or using phones while driving.

Key capabilities:
- Prevents phone use and speaking during driving
- Intelligent emergency handling
- Automatically informs callers of driver status
- Logs behavioural data into a driving profile

Supported real-time driver profiles include:
- Praying
- Sleeping
- Working
- Eating
- Gyming

Callers receive:
- Custom caller tunes
- SMS notifications reflecting driver status

Impact & Recognition:
- Amruta Startup Winner 2021
- Recognized as a life-saving AI system
- Government-issued pilot order for deployment

------------------------------------------------
Patent IN473642 — DopaNet™ (Applied 2022, Granted 2023)
------------------------------------------------

DopaNet is a patented WiFi monetisation infrastructure platform that
converts internet usage into sustainable revenue via targeted advertising.

Core architecture includes:
- Custom router firmware
- Built-in advertisement stack
- Monetisation agents across all devices (phones, TVs, IoT)
- Hyper-local and deep targeting capabilities

DopaNet enables:
- Free or ultra-low-cost internet for users
- Revenue generation for institutions and ISPs
- Sustainable public and rural broadband models

Large-scale collaborations and rollouts are actively underway.
